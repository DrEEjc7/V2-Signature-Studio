📖 How to Use Your Signature

  1. Click "Copy HTML" to copy your formatted signature
  2. Open your email client's signature settings:
    • Gmail: Settings → General → Signature
    • Outlook: File → Options → Mail → Signatures
    • Apple Mail: Preferences → Signatures
  3. Paste using Ctrl+Shift+V (or ⌘+Shift+V on Mac)
  4. Save your signature and start using it professionally!
